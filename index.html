Conlingo - The world's best way to learn Patchélogue

(And notice me if I ever spell it "Conlinguo" again.)

Conlingo is a rip-off version of the world-famous language-learning app, Duolingo. It tends to teach the user my conlang, Patchélogue, in the Duolingo way.

The whole project is under construction and there's only a demo of demos. Either I someday learn to generate pages with random quizzes with JS, of I make every single quiz an independent HTML page.
