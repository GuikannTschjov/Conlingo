Eyki, Keyki and Cake-y

In Patchélogue, both the first and the last letter of a proper noun should be capitalized, like I̱ıᴚĪ⟨EykI⟩ and ꓘḷıᴚĪ⟨KeykI⟩. Those are names of goddess of justice, and goddess of creativeness in their religion relatively. And then there’s an everyday noun ꓘḷıᴚi⟨Keyki⟩ that means “cake”.
