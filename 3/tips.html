Gender but it’s about Consciousness

There are 2 genders in Patchélogue, which is not divided by sеx but by consciousness.

Unlike Spanish or German, a noun’s grammatical gender isn’t fixed. For example: a vehicle can be “conscious” when operated by a driver, a person can be “unconscious” when sleeping hard. That means you don’t memorize things like la mesa or das Mädchen in Patchélogue.

Conscious Unconscious
nż ⅄ṣʌisż ɒı̣ ꓘṣᴚṣɑ żu₉
